BUSINESS INVENTORY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Title: Design and Implementation of Business Inventory Management System

Problem to Solve: Business Inventory Management System

Target Users: Small business owners, Inventory managers

Existing System: There are so many existing systems

1. Introduction

Small businesses play a major role in economic growth, but many still rely on manual methods such as notebooks to manage their inventory. These methods are often inefficient, error-prone, and time-consuming. This project proposes the development of a simple web-based inventory management system to help small businesses manage their products and stock effectively.

2. Problem Statement

Many small business owners track inventory manually, which leads to:
  • Loss of data/items due to poor record keeping
  • Difficulty knowing current stock levels
  • Time-consuming manual processes
  • Errors in sales and stock calculation

3. Aims of the project

The aim of this project is to design and develop a web-based inventory management system that helps small businesses efficiently manage their products, sales, and stock levels.

4. Objectives of the Project

  • Design a simple and user-friendly inventory system
  • Allow business owners to add, edit, and delete products
  • Track available stock quantity in real time
  • Record sales transactions
  • Reduce errors associated with manual inventory management

5. Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is a web application that allows small business owners to manage their inventory digitally. The system will have the following features:

  • Register and log in securely.
  • Add products with price and quantity
  • Update stock when sales are made
  • View current inventory status

6. Scope of the Project

Included
  • User authentication (login and registration)
  • Product management (add, update, delete products).
  • Stock tracking
  • Sales record management
Not included
  • Online payment processing
  • Mobile application version

7. Tools Needed

  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (REACT)
  • Back-end: Node.js
  • Database: JSON / MongoDB, Supabase
  • Code Editor: Visual Studio Code

8. Significance of the Project

This system will help small business owners:

  • Keep accurate inventory records
  • Reduce losses caused by poor stock management
  • Save time and improve productivity
  • Make better business decisions based on real-time data

9. Expected Outcome

At the end of the SIWES program, a functional web-based inventory management system will be developed, tested, and ready for use by small business owners.

10. Conclusion

The Small Business Inventory Management System will provide an efficient and reliable solution to common inventory problems faced by small businesses. The project will also help the student gain practical experience in software development, system design, and database management.
